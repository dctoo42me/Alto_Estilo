About Us

At Alto Estilo, every haircut is a reflection of craftsmanship and passion. Owned and operated by Santos Guerrero, a master barber with roots in Harlingen, TX. Over the years Santos has honed his skills and developed a deep expertise in a wide range of cuts and styles. Whether you’re looking for a classic trim or a modern twist, Santos brings his artistry and precision to every client, ensuring you leave the chair looking sharp and feeling confident. At Alto Estilo, we don’t just cut hair—we create style.
